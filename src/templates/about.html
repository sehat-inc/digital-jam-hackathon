About LeaseAI

LeaseAI is an innovative solution designed to streamline lease contract management using the power of artificial intelligence. We provide tools for both lessees and lessors to efficiently handle contracts, ensure regulatory compliance, and effectively assess risks.

This project is brought to you by Sehat Gang, a student startup group passionate about developing cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems. We are keen on leveraging technology to create impactful and user-friendly applications.

Our mission is to simplify complex processes and empower users with intelligent tools. LeaseAI represents our commitment to innovation and our dedication to providing practical solutions for the leasing industry.
